## Need Help?

If you are stuck on anything — confused by an instruction, not sure what to do next, or something on your computer is not working — **ask your instructor**. That is exactly what they are here for. You will never be bothering them by asking a question.

If your instructor is working with another student, raise your hand or make eye contact and they will get to you as soon as they can.
